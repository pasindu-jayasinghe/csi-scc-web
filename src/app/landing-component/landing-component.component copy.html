Introduction

Currently, the world experiences many catastrophic environmental issues because of the industrialization which cause severe damage to the stability of the environment. Climate change or global warming, air pollution, water pollution, and extinction of species can be described as some of the key issues. Therefore, it becomes crucial to take necessary actions to mitigate these environmental issues by businesses through their active participation. Also, now it becomes a norm among consumers to purchase goods and services from sustainable businesses. Therefore, businesses take several possible actions to mitigate and offset their impacts on environment.
Reforestation or tree plantation become very popular among world business leaders to showcase their environmental consciousness to their peers and valued consumers. Further, a well-planned and managed tree plantation activity can cause many Environmental, Social and Economic benefits for the business and the neighboring community.
Carbon sequestration is one of the key benefits from planting of trees as it will reduce CO2 level in the atmosphere and contribute for reducing global warming. Therefore, businesses are always enthusiastic of knowing how much they have contributed to combat climate change by their plantations projects. And for that here we are introducing Smart Trees.
